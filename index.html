National Energy Optimization Initiative

Energy & Infrastructure

Predictive Scheduling of Maintenance to Mitigate Load Shedding

DVG Investment designed a groundbreaking predictive analytics solution intended to support South Africa's power grid operations. Our machine learning models forecast renewable energy output (solar/wind) using weather forecasting data to predict energy production, then align maintenance windows with periods of high renewable energy availability to balance overall grid performance through predictive supply estimation.

More stable national power supply
Improved renewable energy utilization
Reduced strain on traditional systems
Proposal presented to top South African software company for government collaboration
